Spring Training Travel Planner

Welcome! The Spring Training Travel Planner is a quick and convenient way to use your favorite team's spring training location to find game details, and nearby lodging.


Search for your team's Spring Training location from the list of games displayed to the left of the Google Map. See a game you want to attend? Take your search one step further and select “Search hotels nearby” below your desired game. Doing so will list hotels around the stadium of the game you selected. Happy Spring Training Planning!
